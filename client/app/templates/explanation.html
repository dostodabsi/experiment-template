Explanation

This is a so-called reaction time experiment, which means that you should answer as quickly as possible. The experiment consists of trials, which in turn consist of a short presentation of a picture, followed by a target. The target is either congruent or incongruent. Congruent means that the letters are all the same. Incongruent means that the target letter is surrounded by different letters. Your goal is to press the key that corresponds to the target. In the example below, this would be H for the first two, and S for the second two.

HHHHHHH

SSSHSSS

SSSSSSS

HHHSHHH

If you have pressed the right key, you get a green O as feedback. However, if you have pressed the wrong key or were too slow, a red X will appear on the screen.

There will be a practice trial for you the get the general gist. To demonstrate your understanding, please click the heading Explanation above to proceed with the experiment.
